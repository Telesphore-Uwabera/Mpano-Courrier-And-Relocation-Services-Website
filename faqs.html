FAQS

Moving costs depend on several factors, including:
  • The volume and weight of your belongings
  • The transportation method (air, sea, road, or a combination)
  • Your starting location and destination
  • Special requirements, such as artwork crating
  • Accessibility challenges at your property
  • Additional services requested
To get an accurate quote, provide as much information as possible to our surveyor and operations manager. The assessment will help us tailor the cost to your specific needs.

Every move is unique, so we start with a survey to:
  • Estimate the volume of goods and materials required
  • Understand any specific instructions or requests
  • Assess property access (e.g., narrow gates, height restrictions, lifts, or staircases)
This ensures we can provide an accurate plan and estimate for your move.
